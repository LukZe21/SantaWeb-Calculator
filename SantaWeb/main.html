Santa Claus, the man of the hour during the holiday season, is essentially the world's most famous breaking-and-entering expert with a penchant for cookie theft. Dressed in a stylish red suit and rocking a magnificent white beard, Santa's main gig involves sliding down chimneys to distribute presents. Forget about the logistics of fitting through narrow openings or dealing with pesky home security systems; Santa's got it all figured out. He's the jolly old man who somehow manages to deliver gifts to millions of children in a single night, and we're all just here wondering if his sleigh is powered by Christmas magic or some high-tech propulsion system.
